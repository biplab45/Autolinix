Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy is designed for AutoLinix and explains how information is collected, used, stored and protected when visitors interact with our website, AI assistants, forms, consultations and services. AutoLinix provides AI automation, CRM automation, workflow automation, AI agents, voice agents and custom integrations. We are committed to handling information responsibly and transparently.

Information We Collect

We may collect names, emails, phone numbers, company information, business details, project requirements, communication records, technical information, analytics information, IP addresses, browser data, device information and chatbot interactions. We may also collect information submitted through forms, consultations, emails and support requests.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide services, respond to inquiries, improve website functionality, deliver automation solutions, schedule consultations, improve AI systems, maintain security, comply with legal obligations and improve customer experience. Information may also be used for analytics, reporting, research and service improvement.

Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality, understand user behavior, remember preferences and measure website performance. Users can control cookies through browser settings.

AI Assistant Data

Messages submitted to AI assistants may be processed by third-party AI providers. Users should avoid sharing highly sensitive information through chatbot interfaces. Conversation data may be retained for quality assurance, troubleshooting and service improvement.

Third-Party Services

We may use providers such as hosting services, analytics platforms, Calendly, workflow automation platforms, cloud infrastructure providers, CRM providers and AI providers. These providers maintain their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers, legal authorities when required by law, and business partners involved in delivering services.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including access controls, monitoring and secure infrastructure practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, update, delete or request portability of personal information. Requests may be submitted through our contact details.
