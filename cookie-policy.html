2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly, remember preferences, improve user experience, and provide analytics information.

3. How AutoLinix Uses Cookies

AutoLinix uses cookies to improve website performance, remember user preferences, analyze visitor behavior, enhance security, support marketing activities, and provide a better overall experience.
